Our Programmes

Health & Nutrition

Changing the trajectory of children’s early development by ensuring they are set up on a path of good health and nutrition that aids in their social and educational achievements.

Learning & Development

Supporting the cognitive and age-appropriate development needs of children from the early years through to matric on a continuum of care.

Food Security

Mitigating household hunger and malnutrition through sustainable food gardens and community training in good practices.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Transforming local township economies by promoting inclusive youth-led participation within the economic ecosystem.
