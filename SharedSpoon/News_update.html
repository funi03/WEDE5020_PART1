News & Updates

New Partnership with Golden Loaf Bakery

We are excited to announce our collaboration with Golden Loaf Bakery, a well-known local company dedicated to giving back. Every week, the bakery gives hundreds of loaves of bread, rolls, and other baked items, guaranteeing that no meal goes unfinished in our kitchen. This collaboration not only increases our feeding program, but it also lowers food waste by redistributing leftover baked products to people in need. Community members have expressed enthusiasm for the fresh bread that now accompanies our dinners, making them more satisfying and nutritious.

Upcoming Fundraiser: Community Fun Walk 2025

Get out your walking shoes! Shared Spoon will host its annual 5K Fun Walk Fundraiser on October 12, 2025. This family-friendly event will take place at CCC HALL, uniting residents, businesses, and supporters for a day of exercise, fun, and charity. The proceeds from ticket sales and sponsorships will be utilized to buy food for approximately 200 low-income families. Participants will also receive a free t-shirt. Registration is currently open on our website.
